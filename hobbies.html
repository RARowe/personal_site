In addition to programming, I enjoy playing music on the side. I am the drummer for Joe and the Red Rose. My brother and I also wrote, recorded and produced a full length album as The Red Rose (Joe could not make it to the recording sessions).

I am a novice "photoshopper" (I use GIMP) and enjoy making comical or otherwise silly pictures. Many of which I have used for our concert posters. Others are focused around Oklahoma Sooner sports. This one got me fifteen minutes of fame and became a popular Facebook cover photo for Sooner fans.

I volunteer at homeless shelters. Following college I went on a three month journey around the country working in shelters and blogged about my experiences.

Recently, I have started teaching Sunday school to the high school students at my Church.
